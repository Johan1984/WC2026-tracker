Per-Match Form — opponent-adjusted (optional but recommended)

Enter each of the last 10 results: the opponent's Elo (eloratings.net), goals scored (GF) and goals conceded (GA) in that match. The model rates every result against what an average team would have done versus that same opponent, so a 2–0 over a 1400-rated side counts for far less than a 2–0 over a 2000-rated side. Fill any number of rows (blank rows ignored); filling at least one row for a team overrides that team's quick-mode averages above.
